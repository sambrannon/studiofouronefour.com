Scale Wall

The Scale Wall was designed and built by two of our members while in architecture school. Their then team of 8 worked with local bar & Restaurant owners to provide a new art installation above the main bar at the Hi Hat Lounge in Milwaukee WI.

Original team: Sam Brannon, Ashleigh Fischer, Robert McCaigue, Dustin Koch, Collin Brown, Tim Jurasewicz, Justin Dole, and Joe Buccini

Client

Hi Hat Lounge and Bar (Temporary Installation has since been removed)

Location

Milwaukee WI
