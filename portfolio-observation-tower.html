Observation Tower

Location

Oshkosh WI

The general notion of a journey consists of a beginning and an end, but often neglects the moments that one experiences in between. The conception of an observation tower relies on a switchback stair core capped with an observation deck at the top, setting up a monotonous journey.

The rethinking of the modern observation tower combats the switchback notion through the creation of an irregular path, offering a variety of experiences while one ascends to the top.

Through site analysis, the presence of the lake created a condition that must be dealt with. To address this condition the towers leaning form gestures the lake, highlighting its importance.

A narrative path is created composing scenes along the circulation path, framing views, creating spaces for observation, reflection, and repose along ones journey to the top.
