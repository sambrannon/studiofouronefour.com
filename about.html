Mission

Our mission is simple:
To give great designers the opportunity and resources to continue doing what they love.

Background

We all stem from the midwest, and all hold architecture degrees from UWM's School of Architecture and Urban Planning.

We are designers, and we are all extremely passionate about the built environment.

Our individual skill-sets vary, which gives us a wide scope, but we are collectively very good at:

Architectural rendering, Residential plans, Idea development, & Project conceptualization

Focus

We love designing buildings, doing small projects around town, working on competitions, and Paddy's Pub.

Location

We all met, went to school together, live(d), and became friends in Milwaukee so it has become the central hub for our group. We love the area and love what it has to offer. We are currently in a shared office space downtown offered by the amazing people over at MiKE.

If you have a small scale project (residential project, art installation, interior decor), need to run ideas past someone, or need a suite of graphics to represent your idea, we would be happy to help.

If you don't have a large budget, are still in the early stages of idea development, or just want to support young up-and-comers, we would love to get in contact with you.
